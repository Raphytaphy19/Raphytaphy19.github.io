background

Panceca is a portuguese way of making crepes. it's called panceca because it's a pancake rolled up. Mainly my family makes it with just cheese but other people put all kinds of things. I chose this because it is a weekly usual at my house and it tastes amazing. One tip is not to put to much cheese. Personally I like these somewhat burnt so I like to have mine in the oven a bit more than what it says. I reccomend the burnt ones more than the non burnt.
