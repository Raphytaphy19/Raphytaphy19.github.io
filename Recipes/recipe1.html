background

Pao de Queijo is a traditional brazilian dish that is cheese inside a specific type of bread. The bread is circular and has a lot of air in it. One good tip is not to let them get to burnt at the bottom and remember they must be eaten warm.

the Recipe

My pao de queijo recipe
ingredients amount
large egg 1
canola oil 1/4 cup
milk 2/3 cup
tapioca flour 1 1/2 cups
salt 3/4 teaspoon
shredded cheese 1/2 cup
instructions
Step 1 preheat oven 400 degrees F. Lightly grease a mini muffin tin.
Step 2 blend the egg, oil, milk, flour, and salt until it's smooth. then add the cheese and process it all.
Step 3 stir the batter very well and then pour the mixture into the muffin tin cups nearly to the top.
Step 4 bake for 15-20 minutes until very light golden brown.
Step 5 take out of the oven.
Step 6 wait a minute or two then enjoy.
