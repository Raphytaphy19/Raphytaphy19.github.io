Background

Pão de Queijo is a traditional Brazilian dish that is cheese inside a specific type of bread. The bread is circular and has a lot of air in it. I selected this recipe because my family always makes pão de queijo. Everyone from my grandma to my uncles and aunts all make it. Whenever I have pão de queijo it just has a special place in my heart. One good tip is not to let them get too burnt at the bottom and remember they must be eaten warm. Another tip is that whenever you are making pão de queijo you have to pay close attention to time because if you overcook something or wait too long it could turn out mushy or very hard depending on how you cook it.

The Recipe

My pão de queijo recipe
Ingredient Amount
Large egg1
Canola oil1/4 cup
Milk2/3 cup
Tapioca flour1 1/2 cups
Salt3/4 teaspoon
Shredded cheese1/2 cup
Instructions
Step 1Preheat oven to 400°F. Lightly grease a mini muffin tin.
Step 2Blend the egg, oil, milk, flour, and salt until smooth. Then add the cheese and process it all.
Step 3Stir the batter very well and then pour the mixture into the muffin tin cups nearly to the top.
Step 4Bake for 15-20 minutes until very light golden brown.
Step 5Take out of the oven.
Step 6Wait a minute or two then enjoy.
